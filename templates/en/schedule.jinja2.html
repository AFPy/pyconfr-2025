Registration

PyConFR's sprints, talks and workshops are free.

However, registration is mandatory for everyone, including organization staff, volunteers, speakers and sponsors.

Registration is open until the end of the event, limited to:

  • 200 places for sprints,
  • 1 000 places for talks and workshops,
  • 150 places for the Saturday night dinner.
  • PyConFR Registration

With this registration, it is possible to:

  • reserve t-shirts (€15), also sold during the event with limited stocks,
  • reserve a place for the Saturday night dinner (€30),
  • reserve a place for the guided tours of Thursday and Friday evenings,
  • register children to the Chidren Area on Saturday and Sunday.

⚠️ Ordering t-shirts and booking the Saturday evening party will incur a fee for AFPy. No refunds will be possible after the event.

You can also support AFPy, the association organizing PyConFR, with a donation. If you want to vote during the general assembly on Sunday morning, you must become a member.

PyLadies Lunch

A Pyladies lunch is organised on Saturday noon.

This lunch is reserved for women (cis and trans), trans men, non-binary and agender people who do Python.

Access to lunch is by registration only:

  • PyLadies Lanch Registration

Programming Workshop for Children

A programming workshop for children is organised on Saturday afternoon from 2PM to 5PM, in partnership with MixTeen.

The workshop can accommodate a maximum of 16 children, from 7 to 17 years old.

Access to this workshop is by registration only:

  • Chidren Workshop Registration

Children's Area

A children's area is available free of charge on Saturday and Sunday during PyConFR and is run by Au Joyeux Bazar de Claire.

The children's area can accommodate up to 10 children aged 3 to 12 years old.

Various activities are available for children, including reading, building games, board games, drawing…

Registration is on a half-day basis and can be done on the event registration page:

  • Saturday: 8:45 AM to 12:30 PM and 2 PM to 6 PM
  • Sunday: 8:45 AM to 12:30 PM and 2 PM to 5 PM

⚠️ Meals are the responsibility of parents.

Guided Tours

Guided tours are organised with Repère(s) on Thursday evening and Friday evening.

  • Thursday evening: tour in Lyon old city, end of the tour at 7PM
  • Friday evening: tour "Renaissance investigation", end of the tour at 7:30PM.

Group for the guided tours leaves from the Campus René Cassin at 5:15PM, with Arthur.

If the minimal number of people isn’t reached, you’ll be reimbursed during the event.

Saturday Night Dinner

A dinner is organized on Saturday night, from 7:30 PM to 11:00 PM. The place is located at La Cordée Valmy, 6 place Dumas de Loire, 69009 Lyon.

The dinner is accesible only for the ones who chose the option during their registration.

For the dinner, we’re using a caterer:

  • Still and sparkling water
  • Symples sparkling infusion
  • Iced tea and homemade lemonade
  • Mini bourguignon beef burger, citrus carrot ketchup
  • Club sandwich with vegetable canut brains, roast squash with maple syrup (vegan)
  • Corn polenta, forest mushroom cream and roast oyster mushrooms (vegan and gluten-free)
  • Buckwheat blini, horseradish cream, Dombes trout gravelax (gluten-free)
  • Cumin seed shortbread, parsnip hummus, black sesame (vegan and gluten-free)
  • Organic egg marinated in beetroot, bergamot mayonnaise, Timut pepper (vegetarian and gluten-free)
  • Squash cupcake, squash cream with curry, mustard pickles (vegetarian)
  • Quenelle lyonnaise, Fourme d'Ambert cream, figs (vegetarian)

Other alcoholic drinks can be bought (not included in the price) during the dinner.

During the evening, a live-coding performance, by Ralt144MI, takes place.
