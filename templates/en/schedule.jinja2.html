Registration

PyConFR's sprints, talks and workshops are free.

However, registration is mandatory for everyone, including organization staff, volunteers, speakers and sponsors.

Registration is open until the end of the event, limited to:

  • 200 places for sprints,
  • 1 000 places for talks and workshops,
  • 150 places for the Saturday night dinner.
  • PyConFR Registration

With this registration, it is possible to:

  • reserve t-shirts (€15), also sold during the event with limited stocks,
  • reserve a place for the Saturday night dinner (€30).

⚠️ Ordering t-shirts and booking the Saturday evening party will incur a fee for AFPy. No refunds will be possible after the event.

Registration for the children's area is open until 15 days before the start of the event, i.e. October 14th.

  • Children Area Registration

Registration for the PyLadies Lunch is open until October 27th.

  • PyLadies Lunch Registration

You can also support AFPy, the association organizing PyConFR, with a donation. If you want to vote during the general assembly on Sunday morning, you must become a member.

Children's Area

A children's area is set up during the four days of the PyConFR and is hosted by Babychou Services.

The children's area accommodates a maximum of 18 children, aged 3 to 12 years.

Registration will be open at the same time as event registration and will remain open until 15 days before the start of PyConFR. Priority is given to people running sprints, conferences and workshops.

Saturday night dinner

A dinner is organized on Saturday night, from 7:30 PM to 11:00 PM, at Petit Tigre (5 Rue du Faubourg-National, Strasbourg), only for the ones who chose the option during their registration.

This dinner includes a cocktail buffet with:

  • Mixed charcuterie/cheese boards,
  • Crudités,
  • Quinoa salad,
  • Vegetable wok and soy steaks,
  • Savory and sweet flambé tarts,
  • Two drinks (beer, wine, non-alcoholic drink, coffee, herbal tea).

Other alcoholic drinks can be bought (not included in the price) during the dinner.
